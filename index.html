What are ethics in programming?

Ethics are the moral principles that guide how someone makes decisions. In programming, ethics can often mean choosing between the bad and the worse.


There are some things you should never do:

  • Intentionally write code that is hard to follow
  • Make use of copyrighted code without permission
  • Deny the presence of bugs
  • Hide disputes that may affect the project

Legal Concerns with Website Creation

it is important to understand the leagle concerns with website creation so you ensure you are compling with copyright, accesiblity, & global laws when you publish your website


what to look out for

  • make sure that you do not infringe on anyones copyright
  • make sure you are in compliance for any accesiblity laws
  • make sure that you are acting in accordance with the laws of the countrys you are operating in

copyright

Copyright is a type of intellectual property that protects original works of authorship, for our purposes this mainly refers to source code. in order to get a copyright you need to get it registered if you dont have it registered you can still request that it be taken down as long as your working is in a fixed tangible form


what to look out for

  • did you register you work for copyright
  • did i use anything under copyright without express permission

accesiblity

when designing a website you have to make sure that it is accesible for users with disaplitys. section 508 of the Rehablitation act states that all information technologie be accesible to people with disabilitys if it is not you can sue for discrimination even if its accidental


what to look out for

  • add alt text to images
  • have closed captions for videos
  • ADA compliance regulations

golbal laws

when designing a website you have to understand the laws of the countrys you are operating in. diffrent countrys have diffrent accesiblity, copyright, & data/security laws so its important to make sure you are staying within the boundrys


what to look out for
